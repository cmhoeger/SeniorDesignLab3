About Me

Hello! My name is Brianna and I'm a third year student at the University of Iowa pursing a degree in Computer Science & Engineering and a minor in Cinema. I grew up as a Wildcat in the small town of Durant not far from here with my parents, younger sister, and cats. Now, as a Hawkeye, I get to experience a broader, more diverse learning community far beyond my small-town origins. The skills I've developed at the University of Iowa have given me the opportunity to share what I've learned with others as a coding instructor through my internship with a non-profit called The Coding School. Outside of school and work, my interests & hobbies include reading, drawing, painting, and music.
